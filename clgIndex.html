About college

KV Ranga Reddy Degree College for Women was established in the year 1994, sponsored by Sangam Laxmibai Vidyapeet and affiliated to Osmania University, Hyderabad. The Vidyapeet is exclusively dedicated for the women empowerment. Empowerment of students for their all round development through education is our cherished motto.

College Infrastructure

The college has three blocks; I, II and III. Block I is comprised of all Science classrooms. Block II is comprised of all laboratories. Block III is comprised of all Commerce classrooms. Lecture halls are well furnished with requisite number of dual desks and glass writing boards. Every block has a water cooler fixed with purifier to provide pure drinking water.
